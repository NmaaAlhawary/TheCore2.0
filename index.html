THE CORE 2.0 on HTUX.ORG

TheCore 2.0 Entrepreneurship programs are HTU’s comprehensive innovation ecosystem designed to transform promising ideas into market-leading companies. The ecosystem supports founders through a structured journey with two primary startup entry streams into TheCore, each tailored to a distinct applicant type.

THE CORE 2.0 Application Journey

Venture Studio

Pre-Acceleration

6–12 months

It is a program designed for early-stage founders with ideas or early prototypes, running for 6–12 months, offering intensive support, talent matching, and funding in exchange for equity, with ventures operating under Core Inc until official incorporation.

Accelerator

Pre-Acceleration

6–12 months

Designed for startups with working products and early traction, this 6-month accelerator focuses on scaling customer acquisition, growing the business model, and preparing for seed funding.

Enrollment Steps

  1. 1 Create an account on the HTUx.org.
  2. 2 Activate your account using the link sent to your email.
  3. 3 Register at the Core 2.0 Program.
  4. 4 Complete the Core 2.0 requirements.
  5. 5 Start your journey.
